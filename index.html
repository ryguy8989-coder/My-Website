About Me

My name is Ryan Jiang, and I’m a fourth-year Economics and Mathematics major with a minor in Data Science at Northeastern University. Throughout my time at Northeastern, I have developed a strong passion for analyzing data and solving complex problems, which has shaped my academic and career pursuits.

For my first co-op, I worked at SYSO Technologies, an energy startup that focuses on solar battery management. This role gave me valuable experience in the renewable energy sector, where I worked on projects aimed at optimizing energy storage and distribution. It was an incredible opportunity to apply my analytical skills in a real-world setting and learn more about the intersection of technology and sustainability.

In my second co-op, I joined Wayfair’s Outlet Launch and Scale Team. At Wayfair, I focused on data analysis and operations management, helping to improve the company’s online sales processes. The fast-paced environment and focus on data-driven decision-making gave me a deeper understanding of how analytics can drive business growth and efficiency.

Outside of my studies and co-ops, I’m passionate about sports, especially basketball, and enjoy staying active through hiking and running. These activities help me maintain a healthy balance and give me a chance to unwind and recharge.
